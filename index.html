Why We Do This

Archive MIDI Ramer is dedicated to preserving and organizing MIDI files, ensuring that valuable musical data is not lost over time. This project supports creators by providing a structured and searchable repository.

How It Works

Our local database stores MIDI files sorted by levels based on their complexity. Users can search by metadata such as author or file name, and add new information to the repository.

What You Can't Do

  • Files cannot be permanently deleted; they are archived instead.
  • Unauthorized access or distribution of private files is prohibited.
  • Use the repository responsibly to maintain its integrity.
