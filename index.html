About Us

Archive MIDI Ramer is a community dedicated to preserving and sharing MIDI files. Our goal is to ensure that rare and valuable MIDI files are never lost and remain accessible to those who appreciate their musical and historical significance. We are driven by the idea of creating a space where everyone feels their contributions are valued and safeguarded.

Community Rules

FAQ

What is the purpose of Archive MIDI Ramer?

Our purpose is to be a community that shares and preserves MIDI files without devaluing their uniqueness. We aim to feel needed by other communities and help preserve rare creations for future generations.

What if an author requests file removal?

We archive private files instead of deleting them to ensure they are never lost. These files remain inaccessible without the author's permission.

How do you handle rare or unique MIDI files?

We treat rare files with great care, ensuring they are preserved and accessible to those who truly value their significance.

How can authors benefit from this community?

Authors can use our platform to securely store their works and access them anytime. Public files can inspire others, while private files remain safely archived.
